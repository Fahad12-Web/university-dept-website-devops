About Our Department

Our Legacy

Established in 1990, the Computer Science Department has been a cornerstone of academic excellence and innovation. What began with a small group of passionate educators and students has now grown into a nationally recognized department.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver high-quality education, promote research-driven learning, and prepare students to become responsible professionals, innovators, and leaders in the technology-driven world.

Why Choose Us?

We offer industry-aligned curricula, experienced faculty members, modern laboratories, and a learning environment that encourages creativity, teamwork, and critical thinking.
